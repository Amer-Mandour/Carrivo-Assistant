## Key Performance Metrics

### Vector Search:
- **Embedding Generation**: ~200-500ms (API call)
- **Vector Similarity Search**: ~50-100ms (HNSW index)
- **Total**: ~250-600ms

### Fuzzy Search:
- **Synonym Expansion**: ~1-5ms (in-memory)
- **Similarity Calculation**: ~10-20ms (all roadmaps)
- **Total**: ~15-25ms

### Hybrid Approach:
- **Best Case**: 250ms (vector search succeeds)
- **Fallback**: 275ms (vector fails, fuzzy succeeds)
- **Reliability**: 99.9% (dual fallback system)
